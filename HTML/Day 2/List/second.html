Daily Meal Plan

  1. BreakFast

    • Main Dish: Pancakes
      • Maple Syrup
      • Fresh Berries
      • Whipped Cream
    • Side: Fruit Salad
    • Drink: Orange Juice

  2. Lunch

    • Main Course: Prepare Sandwhich By Your Own
      1. Start with two slices of Sourdough Bread.
      2. Add a layer of Turkey Slices.
      3. Place Swiss Cheese on top.
      4. Finish with Letuce and Tomato Sauce.
    • Side: Potato Chips
    • Beverage: Iced Tea

  3. Dinner

    • Appetizer: Garlic Bread
    • Main Course: Spaghetti Bolognese
      1. First, place the cooked spaghetti.
      2. Next, ladle the bolognese sause over the pasta.
      3. Finally, sprinkle With gated Parmesan cheese.
    • Dessert: Tiramisu
